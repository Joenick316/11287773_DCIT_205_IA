Welcome to the Computer Science Department

Discover the exciting world of computer science at the University of Ghana, Legon. Our department is dedicated to fostering innovation, excellence, and cutting-edge research in the field of computer science.

Programs Offered

Explore the diverse range of programs offered by our Computer Science Department. We provide comprehensive education and research opportunities for students pursuing a career in computer science.

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSc CS)
  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSc IT)
  • Master of Science in Computer Science (MSc CS)
  • Master of philosophy in Computer Science (MPhil CS)
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science

Admissions Information

Considering joining our Computer Science Department? Here are some key details about the admissions process.

  • Application Fee: The application fee is currently GH¢200.00 for Ghanaians and US$110.00 for international students and Ghanaians living abroad.
  • Multiple Results: Yes, you can apply with up to 3 sets of results.
  • Application Deadline: Ensure you submit your application before the specified deadline for the upcoming academic year.
  • Required Documents: Please submit all necessary documents, including academic transcripts and recommendation letters, as part of your application.

Frequently Asked Questions

Application & Submission of Forms

  • How much does the application fee cost?

    The application fee is currently GH¢200.00 for Ghanaians and US$110.00 for international students and Ghanaians living abroad.

  • Can one apply with more than one set of results?

    Yes. You can apply with up to 3 sets of results.

  • Can the application forms get finished before the deadline?

    No. The e-voucher is generated from the Banks’ system and cannot get finished.

  • What programming languages are emphasized in the curriculum?

    Our curriculum emphasizes languages such as Java, Python, C++, and more, reflecting current industry demands.

Entry Requirements

  • What are the general entry requirements for admission?

    An applicant for admission to a degree programme in the Computer Science Department must have at least credits (A1 - C6 in WASSCE or A-D in SSSCE) in English, Core Mathematics, and Integrated Science (for Science applicants) or Social Studies (for non-Science applicants).

  • Do you use Integrated Science or Social Studies in calculating the aggregate for Science students?

    In the calculation of aggregates, Integrated Science will be used for Science programmes and Social Studies for non-science programmes. However, all applicants must have at least credit passes in both Integrated Science and Social Studies.

  • What programming skills are recommended for applicants?

    While not mandatory, familiarity with programming concepts and languages is advantageous for applicants to our programs.

  • Can international students apply?

    Yes, the Computer Science Department welcomes applications from international students who meet the specified admission criteria.
