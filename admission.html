In our undergraduate, graduate, and professional admissions, we actively seek applicants whose strengths, interests, and values align with the mission of the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana, Legon. Embracing the belief that talent and innovative ideas can emerge from diverse backgrounds, our department thrives as a remarkably varied community. Our student body hails from all 16 regions of Ghana and a multitude of countries, fostering a rich tapestry of experiences and perspectives. Many of our students are trailblazers, representing the first generation in their families to pursue higher education. Above all, our students are encouraged to tackle challenging problems without fear, with their education at the Computer Science Department serving as a robust foundation for diverse careers and practical preparation for the complexities of life.

Undergraduate Admissions & Aid

All prospective undergraduate students applying to the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana, Legon, are directed to submit their applications through the designated Office of Undergraduate Admissions. Regardless of their chosen program within the department, applicants will find detailed information about the application process on the undergraduate admissions site. This resource offers valuable insights into the journey of applying to our Computer Science Department and features student blogs that provide a firsthand perspective on life within the vibrant academic community at the University of Ghana, Legon.

Graduate Admissions & Aid

To embark on graduate studies within the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana, Legon, prospective students must initiate their application through the dedicated departmental programs. Each department, including the Computer Science Department, maintains its own application website, where detailed admissions requirements and application procedures are outlined. Aspiring graduate students are encouraged to explore the specific application processes tailored to the Computer Science Department, ensuring a seamless and informed journey towards advanced education and research opportunities

Professional & Executive Education

For professionals in the workforce and learners seeking access to the educational resources of the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana, Legon, without the constraints of a full-time traditional degree program, we provide a diverse array of educational options. Our offerings encompass a spectrum of choices, including online courses, on-campus programs, and hybrid formats that combine the advantages of both. These flexible options cater to individuals eager to enhance their skills, stay updated with industry trends, and engage with the cutting-edge curriculum offered by our Computer Science Department, accommodating diverse learning preferences and lifestyles.
